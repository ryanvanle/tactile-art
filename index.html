Crimson

Its Hue

Crimson is a deep, cool red with a hint of blue, making it darker and more intense than ruby or cherry red. It stands apart from warmer reds like scarlet and vermilion, and is deeper than pink or lighter reds like coral.

Where is it seen?

Crimson can be seen in deep red roses, autumn leaves, or royal clothing. It symbolizes passion, love, power, and intensity, often evoking strong emotions like desire or courage. It can also represent luxury and mystery.
